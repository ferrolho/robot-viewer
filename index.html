Force Ellipsoid

A quadratic approximation of the force polytope. The unweighted version assumes a 2-norm constraint on joint torques:

This produces a smooth shape always inscribed within the polytope.

Torque-weighted

Scales each Jacobian column by the joint's actual torque limit , giving a tighter fit:

The improvement is most visible on robots with asymmetric torque limits (e.g. large shoulder vs small wrist motors). When all joints have equal limits, both modes produce identical results.
